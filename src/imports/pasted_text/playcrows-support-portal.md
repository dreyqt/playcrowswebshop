Create a modern, clean, and professional **Donation / Support Portal** for a game server called:

**PLAYCROWS**
**by Hawk**

The purpose of this page is to allow players to support the Playcrows game server and submit their payment information and receipt for verification.

The design should be **clean, minimal, modern, and unique**, inspired by professional gaming platforms and MMORPG game server websites.

### IMPORTANT DESIGN STYLE

Use **solid colors only**.

Do NOT use:

* Gradients
* Glassmorphism
* Excessive glowing effects
* Overly complicated backgrounds
* Heavy visual effects
* Too many animations

Use a clean solid-color design with strong contrast, modern typography, clean spacing, rounded cards, and subtle borders.

The interface should look professional, trustworthy, and easy to navigate.

Use a dark, premium gaming-inspired color palette with solid colors and a simple accent color that matches the **Playcrows by Hawk** branding.

Keep the design visually interesting through:

* Clean card layouts
* Strong typography
* Solid-color sections
* Clear icons
* Subtle borders
* Simple hover effects
* Smooth transitions
* Excellent spacing

The overall flow should feel like a professional game server payment portal.

---

# STEP 1 — PLAYER INFORMATION

The first section should be:

**Player Information**

Add a short message:

**Before you continue, please enter your Playcrows account information. This allows us to properly identify your account and process your support contribution.**

Add two required input fields:

**Player ID**
Placeholder:
`Enter your Player ID`

**Username**
Placeholder:
`Enter your in-game username`

Add a button:

**Continue to Payment**

The Continue button should only be enabled when both fields are completed.

After clicking Continue, transition the user to the next step.

Keep the entered Player ID and Username stored throughout the entire donation process so the information automatically appears in the final payment summary and receipt submission.

# USER EXPERIENCE

The entire donation process should follow this simple flow:

**1. Player Information**
↓
**2. Choose Payment Method**
↓
**3. Choose Amount**
↓
**4. Complete Payment**
↓
**5. Upload Receipt**
↓
**6. Submit for Verification**

Use a visible progress indicator at the top:

**Player Info → Payment → Amount → Receipt → Complete**

The user should be able to go back to a previous step if they need to correct information, but their entered Player ID, Username, selected payment method, and amount should remain saved throughout the process.

Do not reset their information when navigating between steps.

The final experience should feel smooth and engaging, encouraging players to complete the process without feeling overwhelmed.

The design must be fully responsive and work perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Use **solid colors only**, with a clean dark gaming aesthetic, professional typography, simple icons, subtle borders, and a polished modern interface.

Brand everything around:

**PLAYCROWS**
**by Hawk**

Make the page feel like an official payment and support portal for the Playcrows game server.
